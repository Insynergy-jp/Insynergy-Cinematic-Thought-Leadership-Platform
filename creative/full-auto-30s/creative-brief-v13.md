# Full Auto — Creative Brief v13

## Objective

Create a 30-second native YouTube Short that makes one idea immediately legible: the AI did not fail, and the CEO is not a villain; the accident began because no one designed the boundary at which autonomous execution required human approval.

## Audience

Executives, operators, product leaders, and governance owners who authorize autonomous AI agents and need to distinguish automation from judgment architecture.

## Protagonist and continuity

Use one continuous middle-aged Japanese male CEO across every human shot: short dark hair, charcoal suit, black shirt, no glasses. Preserve the same top-floor executive office, oversized monitor, desk, coffee cup, corporate elevator, smartphone, and cool anthracite-and-electric-blue visual language. The CEO is competent and tired. The omission is plausible, not stupidity.

The six live-action scenes are conditioned by the approval-bound `full-auto-v13` portrait reference set. Exact UI copy, numbers, notifications, and the Decision Structure are deterministic post-production layers. Generated footage must contain blank or abstract screens without readable text.

## Shot 07 facial-performance boundary

Shot 07 is an intense but photorealistic human close-up. The CEO has raised, subtly electric-blue veins across the temple and upper forehead; sharply upturned outer eye corners and a narrow predatory glare; strongly asymmetric twisted lips with a little clenched tooth visible; and the unmistakable impression that a previously concealed violent, feral nature has surfaced through the civilized executive mask. His upper lip forms a restrained snarl, his nostrils flare, and his jaw, cheek, and neck muscles compress with barely contained violence.

The ferocity is psychological, never supernatural. Do not create a monster, demon, animal feature, glowing eye, fang, injury, blood, comedy, lunge, or open-mouth scream. Preserve identity, anatomy, side profile, office reflection, cool grade, and the lower-half UI reserve. His line is low, forceful, and controlled: 「誰が承認した？」

## Runtime and audio

Exact runtime: 30.0 seconds. Eight shots. Three authored Japanese dialogue lines only; no narration and no additional words. Music remains restrained and does not swell. Sound design uses quiet room tone, keyboard and mouse actions, notification tones, subtle process ticks, and low system sounds.
