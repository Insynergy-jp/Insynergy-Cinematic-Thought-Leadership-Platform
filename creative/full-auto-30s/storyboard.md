- Generated visuals contain abstract interface geometry only. All exact copy is added in deterministic post-production.
- The system is neutral, orderly, and non-sentient. Fear comes from unbounded continuation.

The same canonical face is held in a portrait close-up. The neutral response appears: `TASK EXECUTED AS CONFIGURED.` A translucent blue control architecture resolves with Approval, Spending Limit, Escalation, and Decision Boundary, but the Spending Limit connection is missing.

The silent four-stage facial progression is exact:

1. Shock: fixed gaze and momentary stillness.
2. Inward anger: brow and jaw tighten without shouting or lunging.
3. Regret: the eyes soften and become subtly moist.
4. Recognition: the face settles into controlled stillness as the missing boundary becomes legible.

The mouth stays closed or neutrally parted with no speech-shaped movement. Eyes remain anatomically natural blue-green with predominantly warm-white sclerae and no conspicuous redness. No prominent forehead, temple, or neck veins; no exertion flush; no gore, monstrous transformation, or theatrical rage.
